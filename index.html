HackLeChalet

HackLeChalet!

March 29-31st.
48 hours of hacking, in a chalet.
Build things. Get prizes.

What?

HackLeChalet is an hackathon type of event, in a chalet (don't worry there's Internet).

You'll work in small teams, build the coolest project you can, and demo it to your peers.

When?

Start: Friday, March 29th, 10pm
End: Sunday, March 31st, 5pm

We'll organize carpooling from Strasbourg's train station (we'll rent a bus if needed) friday evening and sunday evening. Details will follow as we get informations from attendees.

Where?

La Maison du Kleebach
5 Route du Kleebach
68140 Munster, France

I want to register! What should I do?

Take a ticket here at Eventbrite. we have early bird tickets at 30€ until February XXth. Afher that date the regular price will be 50€. Places are limited to 60 seats so hurry up!

Rules

  • Teams can have from 1 to 5 members
  • You will be judged on what you make this weekend
  • 2 minute demos, 3 minutes for questions
  • Have fun !

Judges will be announced soon...
