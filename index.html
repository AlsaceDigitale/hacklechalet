HackleChalet!

May 24-26th.
48 hours of hacking, in a chalet.
Build things. Get prizes.

What?

HackLeChalet is an hackathon type of event, in a chalet (don't worry there's Internet).

You'll work in small teams, build the coolest project you can, and demo it to your peers.

When?

Start: Friday, May 24th, 10pm
End: Sunday, May 26th, 5pm

We'll organize carpooling from the train stations of Strasbourg, Mulhouse and Colmar friday evening and sunday evening (we'll rent a bus if needed). Details will follow as we get informations from attendees.

I want to register! What should I do?

Take a ticket here at Eventbrite. we have early bird tickets at 30€ until April 29th. After that date the regular price will be 50€. Places are limited to 50 seats so hurry up!

Price includes:

  • 2 dinners, 2 lunches, 2 breakfasts
  • 2 nights in the chalet
  • A lot of fun !

You must bring your own sleeping bag and you'd better bring your own extension cord.

Rules

  • Teams can have from 2 to 5 members
  • Hacks can be hardware or software or a combination of both
  • You will be judged on what you make this weekend (and only this weekend)
  • 2 minute demos, 3 minutes for questions
  • Have fun !

Judges will be announced soon...
